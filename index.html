Takes the LEED

Getz Pharma is the only pharmaceutical company in South Asia to achieve LEED (Leadership in Energy & Environmental Design) Platinum Certification from the U.S. Green Building Council (USGBC).

Getz Pharma is an international research-driven, branded generic pharmaceutical company specializing in the formulation, development, manufacturing, testing and marketing of a wide range of affordable medicines.

We manufacture and market brands trusted by healthcare professionals around the world. We are the only company whose manufacturing facility, in Pakistan, is approved by the World Health Organization, Geneva (WHO). Our Quality Control Laboratory is also the first and only in Pakistan to have been Pre-qualified and approved by WHO.

Our Values

Cultural Preservation

Getz Pharma is devoted to the promotion of cultural heritage in the areas of theater, cinema, music, art, and sports. Efforts that celebrate and promote peace, interfaith, and intercultural harmony are always encouraged

Elevating the Underprivileged

We support projects to reform and renovate public sector educational institutions for the underprivileged. This includes providing learning resources, infrastructure upgrades, and students' educational visits and sporting activities.

Environmental Preservation

Preservation of nature and restoring natural habitats for posterity are our prime concerns. Our efforts include supporting green initiatives that promote sustainable development and environmental awareness.

Health Initiatives

As a leading global research-driven pharmaceutical company, we remain at the forefront of supporting organizations and initiatives to improve the healthcare system's inadequacies and address the community's primary care needs.
